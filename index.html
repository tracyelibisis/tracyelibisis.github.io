We have an announcement.

We're getting hitched!

Come celebrate with us!

Saturday, May 9th, 2015

2PM at Bradley's in Loogootee, Indiana

Previous Next

When and Where? Saturday, May 9, 2015 at 2PM.

There will be a small ceremony, followed by a hot lunch, on the restaurant side of Bradley's Bar and Grill. The ceremony starts at 2PM, and lunch will be served immediately afterward. We will provide fried chicken, green beans, mashed potatoes, corn, and non-alcoholic beverages. You are welcome to purchase any additional food or beverage items off the menu, if you wish to do so.

Note: Bradley's is 21-and-over only. They cannot allow minors anywhere on the premises. We apologize for any inconvenience that this may cause.

What about after? Music, games, etc.

After a brief reprieve to allow for clean-up and such, those who choose to stick around can make their way over to the bar side for an extension of the merry-making. There will be conversation, games, and music! Bradley's has a full P.A. system and an assortment of musical instruments that guests may use (if they promise to be careful with them). Of course, they also have the means to play pre-recorded music. Did we mention that it's a bar?

Another note: The bar can get pretty smoky at times, so you may wish to change clothing beforehand or remove articles that are difficult to wash.

Need help finding the place? How about a map?

Bradley's is located at the junction of US Highway 231 and US Highway 50, and is across the street from Wendy's. There is plenty of parking available. Loogootee is enormous, so feel free to call Justin or Tracy if you need additional assistance.

What else? Miscellaneous information.

  • If we haven't received a confirmation from you yet, please let us know as soon as possible if you plan to attend. We need as accurate a head-count as possible in order to ensure that there is enough food to go around.
  • As far as attire goes, you should wear whatever you are most comfortable in. This is a wedding in a bar - it doesn't get more casual than that.
  • The weather forecast currently suggests that Saturday could be pretty muggy, and we have potential for thunderstorms. Please plan your travel accordingly.
  • In the name of convention, we have been urged to register for gifts. That information can be found here (Amazon) and here (Target).
  • Please don't hesitate to ask any additional questions you may have. See you Saturday!
